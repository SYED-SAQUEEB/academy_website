Important Exam Rules & Guidelines

  • Academic Integrity: All exams must be completed with utmost honesty. Any form of cheating or plagiarism will result in disqualification.
  • Time Limits: Strictly adhere to the allocated time for each exam. Submissions after the deadline may not be accepted.
  • No External Help: During the exam, you are not permitted to use unauthorized resources, communicate with others, or seek external assistance.
  • Technical Requirements: Ensure you have a stable internet connection and a compatible device. AIT Academy is not responsible for technical issues on the student's end.
  • Read Instructions Carefully: Always read all exam instructions thoroughly before beginning. Clarifications should be sought before the exam starts.
  • Submission Procedure: Follow the specified procedure for submitting your exam. Confirm successful submission before closing the exam window.
  • Technical Support: For any technical issues encountered during the exam, immediately contact the provided technical support line.
  • Fair Play: We encourage fair competition and ethical conduct. Any suspicious activity will be investigated.
